# Privacy Policy — Sidebar Toggle

_Last updated: 2026-06-04_

**Sidebar Toggle does not collect, store, transmit, or sell any personal or sensitive user data.**

- **No data collection.** The extension does not gather browsing history, personal information, analytics, telemetry, or any page content.
- **No remote code or servers.** All logic ships inside the extension and runs locally in your browser. Nothing is sent anywhere.
- **Local settings only.** Your preferences — which sites are enabled and what is currently hidden — are saved via the browser's `chrome.storage.sync` API so they sync across your own signed-in browsers. This stays within your browser/Google/Microsoft account and is never accessible to the developer.
- **Limited site access.** The extension only runs on its four supported domains — `*.wikipedia.org`, `old.reddit.com`, `www.youtube.com`, `github.com` — solely to hide their sidebars/top bars by applying CSS. It does not read, transmit, or modify page content beyond that.

This extension complies with the Chrome Web Store Limited Use policy and the Microsoft Edge Add-ons developer policies: data is not sold or transferred to third parties, is not used for any purpose unrelated to the single purpose above, and is not used for creditworthiness or lending.
